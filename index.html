Ingredient list:

Beef:

  • 2-3 lbs flank steak (traditional) or chuck roast

Vegetables:

  • 1 large onion

  • 1 large green bell pepper

  • 4-6 cloves garlic

  • 1 medium red bell pepper

Aromatics & Spices:

  • 2 teaspoons salt

  • 1 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper

  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin

  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano

  • 2 bay leaves

  • 1 pinch cayenne pepper (optional)

Liquids & Tomato Products:

  • 1/2 cup dry red or white wine (optional)

  • 1 cup beef broth or stock

  • 16 oz crushed tomatoes (or 2 (8 ounce) cans tomato sauce)

  • 6 oz tomato paste

Finishing Touches:

  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro (optional, for garnish)
  • 1 cup pimiento-stuffed green olives, sliced

Other, for serving:

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil

  • White rice

  • black beans

  • fried plantains
